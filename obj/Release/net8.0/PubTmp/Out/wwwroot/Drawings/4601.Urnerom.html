VectorDraw Web Library

PAN
 To pan you just have to press the left mouse button, keep it pressed and move the cursor inside the drawing area.

ROTATE SCENE
 To rotate your model around press the middle button of your mouse, keep it pressed and move the cursor around.

ZOOM EXTENTS
 To bring your view on the model's extets, double click inside the drawing area.

ZOOM IN - ZOOM OUT
 You can zoom in and out using the mouse wheel.
